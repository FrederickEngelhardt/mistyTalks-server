      Getting Started Overview
      1. Ensure that Misty is plugged in and rear switch is turned on (or up)
      2. Ensure that your phone is connected to the WIFI and Bluetooth is on
      3. Connect to Misty via Bluetooth (Yale?)
      4. Ensure that your Twilio Account is active and set up properly
      5. Visit the Account Settings section to Enter basic information
      6. Visit the Preferences section to Properly Configure Misty to your Router (?)
      7. Test with an SMS messsage
